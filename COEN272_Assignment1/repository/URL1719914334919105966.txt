      <h2>Free Youth Programs</h2> 
      <p class="center">At the Apple Store, we offer kids a variety of programs tailored just for them. Youth Workshops, Field Trips, and Apple Camp are great ways to get kids&nbsp;thinking, learning, and creating — all while having&nbsp;fun.</p>

   <section class="section section-camp-fieldtrip section-nopad"> 
    <div class="section-content"> 
     <div class="row"> 
      <div class="column large-6 small-12"> 
       <div class="apple-camp"> 
        <figure class="image-retail-learn-youth-camp align-large-left-small-center"></figure> 
        <h3>Apple Camp</h3> 
        <p>Apple Camp is designed to help spark the imagination of kids ages 8 to 12. They’ll learn to make movies by storyboarding ideas, shooting video, and creating an original soundtrack. Or campers can create interactive books complete with their own illustrations and sound effects. At Apple Camp, kids and their creativity are the focus.</p> 
        <h4>Apple Camp will be back in summer&nbsp;2016.</h4> 
        <p><a href="/retail/camp/notify.html" title="Apple Retail Store - Apple Camp">Sign up</a> to be notified when registration is open.</p> 
       </div> 
      </div> 
      <div class="column large-6 small-12"> 
       <div class="field-trip"> 
        <figure class="image-retail-learn-youth-fieldtrip align-large-left-small-center"></figure> 
        <h3>Field Trips for <br class="show-only-for-small">Students and&nbsp;Teachers</h3> 
        <p>Take your students or your fellow teachers on a Field Trip to an Apple&nbsp;Store. It’s an unforgettable learning experience that&nbsp;works to enhance and align with what kids are already learning in the classroom. On their Field Trip, students can create something amazing right on the spot with Apple products. Whatever they create, students will leave with a&nbsp;renewed outlook and motivation for learning. On their own&nbsp;Field Trip, teachers will learn how to enhance their curriculum&nbsp;with the help of Apple products and content.</p> 
        <h4>We can’t wait to see you at the Apple&nbsp;Store.</h4> 
        <p><a href="/retail/fieldtrip/" title="Apple Retail Store - Field Trip">Sign up</a> for your Field Trip today.</p>
