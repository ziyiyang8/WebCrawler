       <h3>Shipping &amp; Delivery</h3> 
       <p>Shipping is free on all orders. Apple uses the best carriers in the business to make sure your order gets to you on time. From the Secure Checkout page you'll see your item description, price and delivery timing. </p> 
       <h4>Free two-day shipping</h4> 
       <p>Free two-day shipping is available on in-stock items. You can see in your bag if your item qualifies. Free two-day shipping is not available on customized Mac, engraved products, and for certain order types, including orders paid for with financing or by bank transfer. Cut-offs apply. See checkout for delivery dates.</p> 
       <h4>Free next-day shipping on iPhone</h4> 
       <p>Free next-day shipping is available on any in-stock iPhone. You can see in your bag if your item qualifies. Cut-offs apply. See checkout for delivery dates.</p> 
       <p>Please note that we do not deliver to P.O. box addresses.</p> 
       <h4>Guaranteed shipping services</h4> 
       <p>Where available, you can also choose a faster delivery method for each item on your order, for an additional fee:</p> 
       <ul type="none"> 
        <li><strong> Courier Delivery</strong> ? Delivers on the same day, within two hours (available during business hours on select items, to certain zip codes)</li> 
        <li><strong> Guaranteed Next Day Shipping</strong> ? Delivers in one business day </li> 
        <li><strong> Guaranteed Next Day Shipping, before noon</strong> ? Delivers before noon the day after the product is available</li> 
       </ul> 
       <p>Delivery times vary according to your selected delivery address, availability of your items and the time of day you place your order. A signature may be required for delivery.</p> 
       <h4>Available to Ship Time</h4> 
       <p>The ‘Available to ship' time is an estimate of when the item will leave our warehouse. For example, if an item displays “Available to ship: 1 business day" the item will leave our warehouse 1 business day after you place your order. While Apple works hard to ship every order as quickly as possible, personalized, custom-configured, and more popular items may have longer ship times. </p> 
       <p>Occasionally, we will confirm ship and delivery times, then learn we are unable to fulfill the order due to low product availability. In these rare cases, we will notify by email of the new ship and delivery times. </p> 
       <h4>Shipping Cost</h4> 
       <p>The cost for shipping is calculated and displayed on the Checkout page before you complete your order. Please note that applicable shipping fees apply to Apple Print Products. </p> 
       <p>You can find delivery costs at checkout, on the Order Confirmation email and online when you view your order on the <a href="http://apple.com/orderstatus">Order Status</a> website. </p> 
       <h4>Puerto Rico</h4> 
       <ul type="none"> 
        <li>Average transit time is 2-5 days.</li> 
        <li>Carriers deliver to Culebra and Vieques twice per week.</li> 
        <li>Overnight shipping is available to some zip codes for an additional cost.</li> 
       </ul> 
       <p class="toplink"><a href="#help-nav">Return to top</a></p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="topic">
       <a name="pickup"></a> 
       <h3>Apple Store Pickup</h3> 
       <p>If this is your first time selecting an Apple Store, our product pages will display <strong>Available for pickup: Check availability</strong>. Click this link, enter the zip code or the city where you'd like to pick up the item, and click <strong>Search Stores</strong> to check availability. </p> 
       <p>If you’ve previously selected an Apple Store, our product pages&nbsp;will display your chosen location. You can click <strong>See more stores</strong> to check pickup availability at a different Apple Store. </p> 
       <p>Once you're ready to purchase, select <strong>Pick up</strong> at Checkout. If you would like to change the pickup location, select <strong>Change location</strong>. Then <strong>Continue</strong>. Items marked <strong>Available Today</strong> will usually be ready for pickup within an hour. You'll receive a notification email when your item is available to pick up. Please wait for this email before coming to the store. </p> 
       <p>If you would like someone else to pick up your items, select <strong>Someone else will pick up these items</strong>. To pick up your items in an Apple Store, you will need to bring the card used for payment, or the order number and a valid photo ID. If you have designated an alternative pickup contact, they will need to bring the order number and a valid photo ID. </p> 
       <p class="toplink"><a href="#help-nav">Return to top</a></p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="topic">
       <a name="next"></a> 
       <h3>What's Next?</h3> 
       <h3>What's Next?</h3> 
       <p>Once your order is placed, we send information about your order by email. The email you receive depends on whether you chose to have the items delivered or picked up. If you have multiple items on your order, you may receive additional emails for each item.</p> 
       <p><strong>Order Acknowledgement</strong></p> 
       <p>The order acknowledgement email provides a summary of the items on your order. If you chose to have an item delivered, the order acknowledgement will display the delivery address and a <strong>Delivers</strong> date. You can click this to view your order status in <a href="http://apple.com/orderstatus">online Order Status</a>. For pickup items on your order, the email will display your selected Apple Store location. If you have designated someone else to pick up the item for you, the email will also display your pickup contact.</p> 
       <p><strong>Shipment Notification</strong></p> 
       <p>If you chose to have an item delivered, we'll send a shipment notification email when the item leaves our warehouse. This email includes the carrier name, tracking number, and a delivery date. You can click the tracking number to track your package on the carrier's website.</p> 
       <p>If you purchase an Apple Store Gift Card by email, we'll send you an email notification once we send the gift card to the recipient.</p> 
       <p><strong>Pickup Notification</strong></p> 
       <p>Once your items are available at the Apple Store, we'll send a pickup notification email.</p> 
       <p><strong>Pickup Reminder</strong></p> 
       <p>We'll send a reminder email if your item is still waiting to be picked up.</p> 
       <p class="toplink"><a href="#help-nav">Return to top</a></p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="topic">
       <a name="text"></a> 
       <h3>Text Notifications</h3> 
       <p>We send updates by text message, so you can stay updated on your delivery or pickup order. Just add your mobile number at checkout to receive these notifications. The number of messages you'll receive will vary depending on the orders you placed.</p>
       <p> </p>
       <p><strong>What to Expect</strong></p> 
       <p>Once your order is shipped or available for pickup, we'll send you a text message. This will include your order number and a link to the tracking or pickup store information. We will also send you texts on the day of delivery or after your item has been delivered, depending on your selected delivery method, country and carrier. You may receive between 1-4 text messages per item on your order.</p> 
       <p><strong>Changing Text Message Preferences</strong></p> 
       <p>You can edit your mobile phone number at any time by signing in to your account and editing the delivery or pickup notification preferences for your order.</p> 
       <p>Your privacy is important to Apple. So we've developed a Privacy Policy that covers how we collect, use, disclose, transfer, and store your information. Visit <a href="http://www.apple.com/privacy">apple.com/privacy</a>. You can also review our Terms of Use at <a href="http://www.apple.com/legal/terms/site.html">apple.com/legal/terms/site.html</a>.</p> 
       <p class="toplink"><a href="#help-nav">Return to top</a></p>

      <div class="topic"> 
       <a name="delivery"></a> 
       <h3>Carrier Delivery Options</h3> 
       <p>Apple chooses which carrier will deliver your shipment, and some of our shipments require a signature. Your Shipment Notification email will tell you whether a signature is required. The carrier may make subsequent delivery attempts before returning the shipment to Apple. Once your order has prepared for shipment or has shipped, we are unable to change the shipping address.</p> 
       <p>If you are unavailable to provide a signature, Apple offers three easy options for you to receive your package.</p> 
       <h4>Option 1: Pre-sign with Apple</h4> 
       <p>There are two ways you can Pre-sign for your shipment: </p> 
       <ol> 
        <li>Click the Pre-sign for this shipment link in your Shipment Notification email </li>
        <br> 
        <li>Visit <a href="http://apple.com/orderstatus"> online Order Status</a> and click the "Pre-sign for this shipment" link</li> 
       </ol> 
       <p>Complete and print the Shipment Release Authorization form. Leave the form on your door for the carrier, prior to 8:00 a.m. on the day of expected delivery.</p> 
       <p>You can specify a preferred delivery location (e.g., front door, back door, immediate neighbor, or building manager). If you provide an address other than the one specified on the package, someone must sign for the package.</p> 
       <p>If you'd like to pre-sign for more than one shipment, simply print, sign, and leave a Shipment Release Authorization for each package.</p> 
       <p>For safety and security reasons, all deliveries are subject to the driver's discretion. If you are unable to access the Pre-sign Form, call Apple Store Customer Service at 1-800-676-2775 and let us help!</p> 
       <h4>Option 2: Sign to release your shipment to your address, an immediate neighbor, or a building manager </h4> 
       <p>If you did not pre-sign for your package through Apple, and you were unavailable to provide a signature, the carrier will leave a Delivery Notice on your door after the first delivery attempt. </p> 
       <p>You can sign the Delivery Notice, specify a preferred delivery location (e.g., front door, back door, immediate neighbor, building manager), and leave the notice on your door prior to the next delivery attempt.</p> 
       <p>If you provide an address other than the one specified on the package, someone must sign for the package.</p> 
       <h4>Option 3: Pick up package at carrier's local facility</h4> 
       <p>After the first delivery attempt, you can request to pick up your package at our carrier's local facility. Call the number on the carrier's Delivery Notice and inform the carrier you'd like to pick up your package in person. </p> 
       <p>To pick up your package from the carrier's local facility, you'll need:</p> 
       <ul> 
        <li>The Delivery Notice</li>
        <br> 
        <li>Government ID (driver's license, passport) that matches the package name and address</li>
        <br> 
        <li>Second ID with your name on it (credit card, social security card)</li> 
       </ul> 
       <p>The carrier can release the package only to the person included on the shipping address.</p> 
       <p class="toplink"><a href="#help-nav">Return to top</a></p>

       <a name="policy"></a> 
       <h3>Shipping Policies</h3> 
       <h4>Shopping in the U.S.</h4> 
       <p>The Apple Online Store US ships items within the continental US, Alaska, Hawaii and Puerto Rico. No shipments can be made to PO Box addresses, United States territories, or addresses outside of the United States. You may not export any products purchased at the Apple Online Store.</p> 
       <h4>Sales to U.S. territories</h4> 
       <p>We are happy to ship your order to a friend or family member residing within the 50 United States if your billing address is within one of the following United States territories:</p> 
       <ul> 
        <li>American Samoa</li> 
        <li>Armed Forces Africa</li> 
        <li>Armed Forces Americas</li> 
        <li>Armed Forces Canada</li> 
        <li>Armed Forces Europe</li> 
        <li>Armed Forces Middle East</li> 
        <li>Armed Forces Pacific</li> 
        <li>Guam</li> 
        <li>Northern Mariana Islands</li> 
        <li>Palau</li> 
        <li>U.S. Virgin Islands</li> 
       </ul> 
       <p>Simply shop at the Apple Online Store U.S. with the payment method of your choice, and we'll ship the order to any address within the 50 United States.</p> 
       <p>We regret that we cannot ship orders placed on the Apple Online Store U.S. to addresses outside of the 50 United States. Let us help you <a href="http://www.apple.com/buy/">find an Apple reseller</a> to assist you in purchasing Apple products locally.</p> 
       <h4>Shopping outside the U.S.</h4> 
       <p>There are more than 30 Apple Online Stores in countries around the globe. If you would like to purchase Apple products from outside the U.S., please see our <a href="http://www.apple.com/shop/Catalog/US/Images/intlstoreroutingpage.html">International Store Directory</a> to find out if an Apple Online Store serves your country.</p> 
       <h4>Shopping for friends and family who reside outside of the U.S.</h4> 
       <p>Would you like to buy a gift for a friend or family member residing in a different country? Visit the <a href="/shop/help/gifting" data-s-object-id="9096153c27f8406d05571b450926aab4" data-evar1="AOS: help/shipping_delivery | center-2 | Astro Link | 0 | AOS: help/gifting" data-evar30="help/shipping_delivery/center-2">Gifting</a> section of online Help for more information.</p> 
       <h4>Sales to U.S. military personnel stationed abroad</h4> 
       <p>If you would like to ship to an APO/FPO address, please see our <a href="http://www.apple.com/shop/open/shipping_apo" target="new">APO/FPO</a> page.</p> 
       <p>Apple's products may be found at your local PX/BX AAFES store. Please contact <a href="http://www.aafes.com/">AAFES</a> to find out if your base carries Apple products.</p>

   (function (window, $, as, can, undefined) {

            can.view.attr("data-focus-when", function( el, attrData ) {
                var handler = function(ev, newVal, oldVal){
                    if (newVal) {
                        setTimeout(function() { el.focus(); }, 1000);
                    }
                };

                var attrValues = el.getAttribute("data-focus-when").split(',');
                var itemsFocusInCompute = [];
                for(var i=0; i<attrValues.length; i+=1){
                    var focusInCompute = attrData.scope.compute(attrValues[i]);
                    focusInCompute.bind("change", handler);
                    itemsFocusInCompute.push(focusInCompute);
                }

                $(el).bind("remove", function(){
                    for(var i=0; i<itemsFocusInCompute.length; i+=1){
                        itemsFocusInCompute[i].unbind(handler);
                    }
                });
