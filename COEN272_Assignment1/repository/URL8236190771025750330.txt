     <h1>Apple AC Wall Plug Adapter Recall Program</h1> 
     <div id="content" class="box"> 
      <div id="section"> 
       <div class="column first"> 
        <div class="sidebar"> 
         <div class="padtop30"></div> 
         <img src="http://images.apple.com/support/assets/images/products/ac-wall-adapter/apple_logo.png" alt="" align="center" style="width:130px; height:130px; display: block; margin: 0 auto;"> 
         <div class="padtop10"></div> 
         <select style="display: block; margin: 0 auto;" id="country_nav"> <option selected>Please select a Country</option> <option value="/support/ac-wallplug-adapter/other_countries.html">Other countries</option> </select> 
        </div> 
       </div> 
       <div class="column last"> 
        <div id="welcome"> 
         <p>Apple has determined that, in very rare cases, the two prong Apple AC wall plug adapters designed for use in Continental Europe, Australia, New Zealand, Korea, Argentina and Brazil may break and create a risk of electrical shock if touched. These wall plug adapters shipped from 2003 to 2015 with Mac and certain iOS devices, and were also included in the Apple World Travel Adapter Kit.</p> 
         <p>Customer safety is always Apple's top priority, and we have voluntarily decided to exchange affected wall plug adapters with a new, redesigned adapter, free of charge. We encourage customers to exchange any affected parts using the process below.</p> 
         <p>Note: Other wall plug adapters, including those designed for Canada, China, Hong Kong, Japan, United Kingdom, and United States and Apple USB power adapters are not affected by this program.</p> 
         <h2>Identifying your wall plug adapter</h2> 
         <p>Compare your adapter to the images below. An affected wall plug adapter has 4 or 5 characters or no characters on the inside slot where it attaches to an Apple power adapter. Redesigned adapters have a 3-letter regional code in the slot (EUR, KOR, AUS, ARG or BRA).</p> 
         <p>&nbsp;</p> 
         <table width="700" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="2" border="0"> 
          <tbody> 
           <tr> 
            <td align="center"><b>Affected</b></td> 
            <td align="center"><b>Redesigned</b></td> 
           </tr> 
           <tr valign="top"> 
            <td align="center"><img src="http://images.apple.com/support/assets/images/products/ac-wall-adapter/affected_adapter.jpg" alt="Affected adapter detail" style="width:270px; height=200px; margin: 0 auto 0 70px;"></td> 
            <td align="center"><img src="http://images.apple.com/support/assets/images/products/ac-wall-adapter/redesigned_adapter.jpg" alt="Redesigned adapter detail" style="width:270px; height=200px; margin: 0 auto 0 70px;"></td> 
           </tr> 
          </tbody> 
         </table> 
         <br> 
         <br> 
         <h2>Affected Adapter Prong Types</h2> 
         <table width="700" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="5" border="1"> 
          <tbody>

            <td align="center" width="25%"><img alt="Australian/Argentinian Adapter" src="http://images.apple.com/support/assets/images/products/ac-wall-adapter/aus_adapter.png"></td>

         <p>Note: The countries and regions listed are some examples of supported locations for that adapter. Adapters may be used in additional countries. </p> 
         <br> 
         <h2>Exchange Process</h2> 
         <p>Please choose one of the following options below. We will need to verify your Mac, iPad, iPhone or iPod serial number as part of the exchange process so please find your serial number in advance. <a href="https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT204308">Finding your device serial number</a> is easy. </p> 
         <ul class="square"> 
          <li>Exchange your adapter(s) at your local <a href="#cas,caller:rcon,pfc:PFC6004,cid:SC0999,sid:21335">Apple Store</a> (Genius Bar reservation recommended) or an <a href="https://locate.apple.com">Authorized Apple Service Provider</a>.</li> 
          <li>Contact <a href="#cas,pfc:PFC6004,cid:SC0999,sid:21335">Apple Support</a>.</li> 
         </ul> 
         <br> 
         <p>Customers who purchased a World Travel Adapter Kit should use&nbsp;one of the options&nbsp;above to exchange their affected parts.</p> 
         <br> 
         <h2>Additional Information</h2> 
         <p>This program does not affect your statutory or warranty rights.</p> 
         <p>If you believe you have paid for a replacement due to this issue, <a href="#cas,pfc:PFC6004,cid:SC0998,sid:21336">contact Apple</a> regarding a refund.</p> 
         <p><a href="https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT204178">Learn more</a> about using Apple power adapters, cables, and duckheads with Apple products.</p>
