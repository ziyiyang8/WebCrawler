And a True Tone flash matches the ambient light for more true-to-life colors and natural-looking skin tones.</p> 
         <h4 class="subsection-subheadline"> 
          <figure class="progressive-image progressive-image-animated icon-icloud"></figure> iCloud Photo Library. </h4> 
         <h5 class="subsection-subheadline">All your photos and videos, always with you.</h5>
