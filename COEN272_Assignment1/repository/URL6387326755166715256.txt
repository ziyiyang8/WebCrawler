      <div class="large-11 large-centered text-centered"> 
       <h3 class="typography typography-subsection-subheadline">Mac surges while support costs drop at IBM.</h3> 
       <p><button class="drawer-trigger" data-analytics-click="prop3:learn more - ibm">Learn more</button><span class="icon icon-zoomalt span-icon"></span></p> 
      </div> 
     </div>

    <!-- section-content-ibm-intro --> 
    <div id="drawer-content" class="section-content section-content-ibm-drawer"> 
     <div class="row row-ibm-content"> 
      <div class="column large-6 large-offset-1"> 
       <div class="ibm-copy-item"> 
        <p><strong>Employees popularize Mac and reap productivity.</strong></p> 
        <p>Employees have made Mac the overwhelming technology choice&nbsp;at IBM. Inspired by how Apple deploys Mac internally, IBM followed suit — shifting to a modern approach to deploying, managing, and supporting devices.</p> 
        <p>With Mac, employees are up and running quickly with no need&nbsp;for&nbsp;complex instructions or in-depth training — rapidly benefiting from the usability of OS X, a platform that they are already familiar with from personal use at home.</p> 
       </div> 
       <div class="ibm-copy-item"> 
        <p><strong>Modern services enable scalability at speed.</strong></p> 
        <p>To implement the Mac@IBM program, IBM created services to roll&nbsp;out each Mac in a day’s turn, all within a highly secure environment. This modern approach to deployment allowed IBM&nbsp;to roll out up to 250 new Mac notebooks per day, adding to the&nbsp;over 130,000 Apple devices already in use at the company.</p> 
        <p>Behind the scenes was an IBM-developed zero-touch deployment process, which takes advantage of the Apple Device Enrollment Program and device management from JAMF. </p> 
        <p>Each Mac is distributed to end users in a shrink-wrapped box, without imaging or any kind of expensive staging services. And built-in tools in OS X secure and protect corporate data without the need for IT to license additional third-party software.</p> 
       </div> 
       <div class="row-ibm-quote ibm-copy-item"> 
        <figure class="quote quote-narrow"> 
         <blockquote class="quote-copy"> 
          <p>Between lower support costs and higher residual values, every Mac we deploy makes and saves money for IBM.</p> 
         </blockquote> 
         <figcaption class="quote-credit rmv-itl">
          Fletcher Previn, VP of Workplace as a Service, IBM
         </figcaption> 
        </figure> 
       </div> 
       <div class="ibm-copy-item"> 
        <p><strong>Light support yields big benefits.</strong></p> 
        <p>With the Mac@IBM program in place, the company is realizing a dramatic reduction in help desk costs, better coverage ratios for support staff, and higher satisfaction ratings from employees. Users are more engaged, more productive, and need less support from the help desk.</p> 
        <p>And now, IBM is bringing these same services and best practices&nbsp;to&nbsp;companies around the world.</p> 
        <p><a href="http://www-935.ibm.com/services/us/en/it-services/enterprise-mobility/mms4osx/index.html" class="icon icon-external" target="_blank">Learn more about services for Mac from IBM</a></p>
